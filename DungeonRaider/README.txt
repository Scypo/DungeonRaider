WSAD - walking
LMB - shooting
R - reload
ESC - BuyMenu
F11 - Fullscreen

Each room you clear gives you a point that you can spent on upgrades like:
-health boost,
-shield boost,
-weapon boost,
-medkit for healing.
Each room has randomly spawned enemies with random weapons such as
-AssaultRiffle,
-SMG,
-DMR,
-Sniper
based of those weapons enemies have different stats like health, shield, etc.
The goal of the game is to clear as many rooms as you can before dying.
